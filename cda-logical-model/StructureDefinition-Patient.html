Logical Model: Patient (CDA Class)

Official URL: http://hl7.org/fhir/cda/StructureDefinition/Patient Version: 2.1.0-draft1
Active as of 2023-02-07 Computable Name: CDAR2.Patient

A LivingSubject as a recipient of health care services from a healthcare provider. The patient is the player; the provider is the scoper.

Usage:

  • This Logical Model Profile is not used by any profiles in this Implementation Guide
